OUR STORY

Redefining Roadside Assistance in Kuwait.

Founded in 2021, My Sayartee was built on a simple premise: premium automotive care should come to you. Operating from the Shuwaikh Industrial Area, we replaced the traditional waiting room with a 24/7, GPS-enabled mobile fleet.
